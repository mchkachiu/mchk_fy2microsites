Plastic Pollution

More than 8 million tons of plastic is dumped into our oceans every single year


Plastic- its everywhere.




Plastic is a part of our everyday lives. Its in our grocery stores, our restaurants, our toiletries, toys, furniture, clothes. But most importantly, its in our oceans. We use it to package food, and to bottle products. To make dinnerware and to make toys. Plastic has helped us to manufacture, package, and ship goods more easily, for less money and safer.
