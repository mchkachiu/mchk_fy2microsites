Things About Malvern College Hong Kong: The True Colours

Background

Malvern College Hong Kong is an international day school that was created on 2018 June 18th and was established on August 2018. It has attracted about 800 pupils to this school and the target is about 1100 pupils. The school facility itself has a cafeteria (called "The Grub"), a chain of science laboratories, a school auditorium (called "Big School), a two floored library, a Sports Hall, and a Skypitch for football/soccer and floor hockey, which in total is about 7 floors. The 6th floor is still currently in construction, and the school strongly recognizes ettiquette and tidiness. The school's motto is "Sapiens qui prospict", and is a full British traditional school.

The Experience of Malvern

The expierence of Malvern is a British experience where students have to where a formal uniform with a tie, but also tuck in their shirts as a sign of dressing smartly. But here are the following things that Malvern follows the British Tradition.

  • The specific classrooms in Malvern are named after a bunch of symbolic terminology, like the cafeteria is named "The Grub" after some war bunker at the time.
  • Malvern celebrates a lot of British traditional stuff, like Rememberance Day, which is a day to remember the deaths of Malvern students in World War 1.
  • The headmaster, Dr Lister would constantly check your shoes, since if your shoes are polished it would represent you being smart.
