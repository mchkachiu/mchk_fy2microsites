The Power of Microsites

FY2 MYP Design - MCHK


S0.1 - Setup your Learning Studio

  1. Step 1

    Your MCHK Learning Studio is a Google Site.

    All MCHK MYP pupils have a Google Site called your Learning Studio. It aims to be a space for you to capture, reflect on and share your learning.

    Find my Learning Studio site on Google Sites

  2. Step 2

    Find the MYP2-FY2 > FY2 Design page.

    Create a new page by clicking on the New Page button

    Make this page "2D1 - Microsites", which is the name of the current unit. Make sure you place the page under the FY2 Design page.

  3. Step 3

    Create an Announcement Page titled "Journal" under your "2D1 - Microsites" page.

  4. Step 4

    Create a web page called "Skills - Web Development" under your "2D1 - Microsites" page

  5. Step 5

    Go to your "2D1 - Microsites" page

    Copy the URL (address) to the page and paste it next to your name on this register.

  6. Return to Unit Overview
