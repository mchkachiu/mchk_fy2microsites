Hearthstone Info

Hearthstone is a well known game that has been released for more than 6 years. It still updates to this date and has given out a lot of content to the players who enjoy this game. Today I am going to explain to you the major concepts of Hearthstone itself and how they work.

How Hearthstone works

Hearthstone is a traditional card game that is very well known from Blizard. For Hearthstone, the game's mechanics is mainly a turn-based card game where players use a hero to combat against other players. The motive is to use your hero and reduce the enemy player to 0 health. You do this by using "minions" to help reduce down health. The first person to do so wins.

Classes


There are different types of classes for your hero. In total, there are 9 classes, and I will list them down below.

Mage

Mages are wizards in the human world where they focus mainly on spells most of the time. They form a club of wizards called the Kirin Tor, which is only for the best of the best. Their Hero Power is Fireblast, 1 damage for 2 mana. Subsequent Mages include Archimage Antonidas, Medivh (who became corrupted in the end), Khadgar etc.

Hunter

Hunters, by it's name are also humans in the human world that would hunt beasts (type of minion) and would cut down their hide to make skins or weapons. Their Hero Power is Steady Shot, 2 damage for 2 mana. Subsequent Hunters include Rexxar, Alleria Windrunner, Beastmaster Leoroxx etc.

Warrior

Warriors are hard core fighters that use weapons in order to fight. They are mainly fighters, and are the major generals on field. Their Hero Power is Armor Up, which generates 2 Armor for 2 Mana. Subsequent Warriors include Garrosh Hellscream, Grommash Hellscream, Deathwing (in his mortal form) etc.

Shaman

Shaman tribes are mainly people who harness the elements upon others. They are mainly elementalists who summon totems in order to support themselves and punish others who have threatned them. The Shaman's Hero Power is Totemic Call, which generates a Totem for 2 Mana. Subsequent Shamans include Thrall, Lady Vashj, The Thunder King etc.

Druid

Druids are elfs that harness nature to heal wounds and protect life. Master Druids can shapeshift really well and are in deep tune with animal spirits. The Druid's Hero Power is Shapeshift, which gives 1 Attack and 1 Armor for the Druid, all for 2 Mana. Subsequent Druids include Malfurion Stormrage, Lunara and Dame Hazelbark.

Priest

Priests are either humans or night elves that deal healing as their main power. Priests are usually able to devote to spiritual guides. The Priest's Hero Power is Lesser Heal, being able to heal 2 Health for 2 Mana. Notable examples of Priests involve: Tyrande Whisperwind, Anduin Wrynn and Madame Lazul.
